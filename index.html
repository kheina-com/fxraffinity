Embed furaffinity posts directly into your chat of choice by replacing "furaffinity.net" with "{hostname}" in the post url.

Example:
https://www.furaffinity.net/view/42140481 becomes https://{hostname}/view/42140481


Advanced:
If you are having an issue where a thumbnail is being returned and you want the fullsize image loaded instead, you can add ?full to the end of the url to forcefully load the fullsize image.

Example:
https://{hostname}/view/42140481?full
